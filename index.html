About Us

At DUORECRUIT, a PS Universal company, we specialize in delivering best-in-class recruitment and staffing solutions across diverse industries. With a deep understanding of talent dynamics and business needs, we connect organizations with top-tier professionals who drive success.

Our expertise spans Technology, Analytics, Finance, Risk, and other enabling functions, ensuring that businesses find the right talent for every role. Whether you are looking for highly specialized professionals, large-scale hiring solutions, or entry-level talent, DUORECRUIT has the experience, network, and capability to deliver.

We are committed to integrity, agility, and excellence, making us the trusted recruitment partner for companies seeking scalable, high-quality talent solutions.

Our Services

Contingent Search

We provide targeted recruitment solutions for permanent and contract roles, ensuring companies have access to pre-vetted, high-quality candidates that align with their specific needs.

Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO)

Our end-to-end recruitment solutions streamline hiring, cut costs, and boost efficiency with tailored full-cycle or modular RPO services.

Campus Hiring

We partner with top universities and institutions to identify and onboard fresh talent with the right skills and potential, helping businesses build their future workforce.

Train & Hire

Our innovative Train & Hire model bridges skill gaps by training candidates on job-specific skills before deployment, ensuring organizations get work-ready professionals from day one.
